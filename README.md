# PlanOS
A lightweight, and local personal efficiency and time-management system tailored for university students. Built purely with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

## Core Features
- **Dual-Mode Timetable:** Seamlessly switch between a traditional vertical calendar view and a horizontal Gantt-style timeline to visualize your week effectively.
- **Dynamic Scheduling:** Assign multiple time slots to a single course or event, handling complex academic timetables with ease.
- **Advanced Task Management (Drag & Drop):** Manage long-term projects and short-term assignments with dedicated modules. Enjoy smooth, native drag-and-drop functionality to reorder tasks and projects intuitively.
- **Color Coding System:** Organize your schedule visually with a curated palette of pastel themes for courses and events.
- **Local-First Architecture:** Zero backend, zero latency. All your data (schedules, tasks, and configurations) is securely stored in your browser's `localStorage`, ensuring complete privacy and offline availability.
- **Integrated Focus Mode:** A built-in Pomodoro timer helps maintain deep work states, directly accessible from your dashboard.
